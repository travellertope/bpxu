# BPU Member Portal — Product Overview

*A plain-English guide to everything that has been built. Written for non-technical team members.*

---

## What is this?

The BPU Member Portal is a web application that lives at **app.blackprofessionals.uk**. It is the digital home for BPU members — a private, personalised space where they can manage their career, find jobs, access courses, book mentors, and get feedback on their CV.

It also powers a second product — **PAIRED** — which runs at pairedbybpu.uk and handles the mentorship side of the platform.

Both products are part of the same system, built and maintained together.

---

## Who uses it?

There are four types of users:

| User type | What they do |
|---|---|
| **Free members** | Browse jobs, courses, and events; use basic CV tools; access mentor directory |
| **Pro members** | Everything a free member can do, plus AI job matching, CV upload & parsing, expert CV reviews, mentor compatibility scores, and weekly job digests |
| **Employers** | Post job listings, track applications and engagement, manage their company profile |
| **Mentors** | Listed in the PAIRED directory; receive booking requests from members |

## The Two Products

### 1. BPU Member Portal (app.blackprofessionals.uk)

This is the main member experience. After signing in, members land on a personalised dashboard with six sections:

---

### Dashboard — Overview

The first thing a member sees after logging in.

- A welcome message with their name and career field
- A prompt to upgrade to Pro (for free members)
- **Three stat cards** showing at a glance:
  - How many courses they are enrolled in (and how many are in progress)
  - How many upcoming events they have registered for
  - How many professional CV reviews they have received
- A preview of their top job matches
- A quick snapshot of their CV Clinic status

---

### Dashboard — CV Clinic

Three distinct tools, shown as tabs so members understand what each one does:

**1. AI Analysis** *(free for all members)*
The member enters a job title they are targeting, pastes in a job description (optional), and uploads their CV. The AI reads both and gives back:
- A score out of 100 showing how well the CV matches the role
- A list of strengths in the CV
- A list of areas that need improvement
- A single top recommendation for what to fix first

This does not save the CV to their profile — it is just instant feedback.

**2. Upload & Parse** *(Pro members only)*
The member uploads their CV as a PDF. The AI reads it and automatically fills in their BPU profile — work history, education, qualifications, certifications, and languages. The CV is also stored so recruiters and mentors can view it.

**3. Expert Review** *(Pro members only)*
The member requests a written critique from a real BPU recruiter. The recruiter reads the CV and writes personalised, detailed feedback covering structure, language, keywords, and presentation. Typically returned within 3–5 business days. Past reviews are saved and can be re-read at any time.

---

### Dashboard — Jobs

A feed of job opportunities matched to the member's profile. For Pro members, each job shows a compatibility score. Members can:
- Apply directly through the portal (for inbound BPU jobs)
- Be sent to a partner's website (for partner-listed jobs)

---

### Dashboard — Courses

Shows only the courses the member is enrolled in — not every course on the platform. For each enrolled course:
- A thumbnail image and category
- The provider and duration
- A progress bar showing how far through the course they are
- A button to continue or review the course

Members who have not enrolled in any courses yet are prompted to browse the full course catalogue.

---

### Dashboard — Events

Shows only events the member has registered for — not all BPU events. Each event shows:
- Whether it is online or in person
- Whether it is free or paid
- The date, time, and venue
- Past events are visually faded out

Members can browse all upcoming events from a dedicated public page.

---

### Dashboard — My Profile

A full profile editor covering everything BPU holds about the member:
- Personal details (name, phone, date of birth)
- Demographics (gender, sexuality, ethnicity, disability status, immigration background)
- Career information (employment status, industry, field of expertise, years of experience, skills, professional bio)
- Location (country, region within the UK, city)
- LinkedIn profile link

There is also a read-only section showing what the AI extracted from their uploaded CV — a structured timeline of their work history, education, and certifications.

Pro members can also set their email preferences here, including opting in to a weekly job digest sent every Monday.

---

## Public Pages (no login required)

These pages are accessible to anyone — members, employers, or first-time visitors.

---

### Job Board (/jobs)

A searchable directory of all current job listings. Visitors can filter by:
- Keywords (job title or company name)
- Industry (13 categories)
- Job type (apply directly through BPU vs. external partner listing)
- Remote only
- Employment type (full-time, part-time, freelance, contract, internship)

Each job card shows the company logo, title, location, salary range, and when it was posted. Clicking a job opens the full description, company information, and an apply button.

For direct (inbound) jobs, applicants fill in a guided application form including their CV, a cover letter, and any screening questions set by the employer. For partner jobs, they are tracked and sent to the employer's own site.

---

### Courses Board (/courses)

A browsable catalogue of all courses available through BPU partners.

**Description shown to visitors:**
> *Discover a diverse range of courses that cover various industries and subjects, providing you with the tools to thrive in your career. From leadership development to digital marketing and everything in between, our free courses offer valuable insights and practical knowledge. All courses are fully accredited and recognised throughout the UK and internationally.*

Visitors can filter by:
- **Discipline** — the subject area of the course (e.g. Leadership, Marketing, Technology)
- **Country** — the country the course is accredited or relevant to
- Keyword search

Each course card shows the course image, category, country tags, provider, duration, and a short description. Clicking "View course" takes the visitor to the partner's course page.

---

### Events Board (/events)

A calendar of all upcoming BPU events displayed in a **timeline layout** — grouped by month, with each event shown as a card on a vertical timeline.

Visitors can filter by:
- Online vs. In Person
- Free events only
- Keyword search

Each event shows the date, time, venue, format, cost, and a short description. A "Register" button links to the registration page.

## PAIRED — Mentorship Platform (pairedbybpu.uk)

PAIRED is BPU's mentorship product. It connects ambitious Black professionals with experienced mentors across the UK. It shares the same login system as the main portal.

---

### PAIRED Home Page

The public-facing landing page explains what PAIRED is and how it works:

- **AI-powered matching** — the system analyses a member's BPU profile to suggest mentors whose experience aligns with where they want to go
- **Easy scheduling** — members book sessions directly from a mentor's profile, no back-and-forth emails
- **UK-wide community** — every mentor is a verified Black professional in the UK
- **Career-specific guidance** — filter by industry, role, and skills

A four-step "How it works" section guides new users from creating an account through to their first session.

---

### Mentor Directory

A searchable grid of all active mentors. Visitors can search by name, role, or skill and filter by industry. Each mentor card shows:
- Their name and initials avatar (in a consistent colour unique to them)
- Their field of expertise and industry
- Their years of experience
- Up to three skill tags
- A "View profile" button

---

### Mentor Profile

A full page for each mentor showing their bio, skills, industry, years of experience, and available booking slots. Members can book a free 1-on-1 session directly from this page by selecting a date, choosing a time slot, and adding a note about what they would like to discuss.

---

### PAIRED Dashboard (my sessions)

A private page for each member showing their mentorship activity:

- **Stats**: upcoming sessions, past sessions, total hours
- **Upcoming bookings**: shown as purple cards with the mentor/mentee name, date, time, and status (pending or confirmed)
- **Past sessions**: a history of completed sessions with a "Book again" shortcut

On the right side:
- **Pro members** see a panel of their top 3 AI-matched mentors, ranked by compatibility with their profile
- **Free members** see an upgrade prompt explaining what AI matching unlocks

---

### Become a Mentor

A separate application form for experienced professionals who want to give back. Applicants fill in their job title, employer, years of experience, area of expertise, availability, whether they have mentored before, and their motivation. Applications are reviewed by the BPU team before the mentor is listed in the directory.

---

## Employer Portal (/employer)

A separate section for organisations who want to post jobs to the BPU community.

### What employers can do:

**Post jobs** — create a listing with title, full description, location, employment type, industry, salary range, remote option, and optional screening questions for applicants.

**Choose job type:**
- *Direct (inbound)* — applicants apply through the BPU portal and their details land in the employer's dashboard
- *Partner (outbound)* — the listing links out to the employer's own careers page; BPU tracks click-through data

**Track performance** — each job shows impression counts (how many people saw it), click counts, and application counts.

**Manage their profile** — upload a company logo, add a website, tagline, social links, video, and company description. This branding appears alongside their job listings.

## Account & Registration

### Member registration

New members go through a friendly 5-step sign-up process:

1. Create an account (username, email, password)
2. Personal details (name, phone, date of birth, gender, sexuality)
3. Background (education level, ethnicity, immigration background, disability)
4. Career (employment status, industry, field of expertise, years of experience, skills)
5. Location & bio (country, UK region, city, professional bio)

All steps are optional where marked — members can always fill in more later from their profile page. The form is protected by Google reCAPTCHA to prevent spam.

### Employer registration

Employers register with their company name, contact name, work email, and a password. Once registered, they have access to the employer dashboard to post and manage listings.

## Pro Membership

Pro membership unlocks a set of advanced tools:

| Feature | What it does |
|---|---|
| AI job matching | Daily personalised job recommendations ranked by how well each role fits the member's profile |
| CV upload & AI parsing | Upload a PDF CV and have the AI automatically populate the member's profile |
| Expert CV review | A real BPU recruiter writes a detailed, personalised critique of the member's CV |
| Mentor compatibility scores | AI-generated percentage scores showing how well each mentor aligns with the member's career goals |
| Weekly job digest | A curated email every Monday with the top matched jobs |

Free members see these features in the interface but with an upgrade prompt — they can see what they are missing before deciding to upgrade.

Upgrading is handled through the main BPU website (WooCommerce subscription). Once payment is confirmed, Pro access is granted automatically.

---

## Notifications & Automated Emails

The platform sends automatic emails in the following situations:

- **Welcome email** — when a new member registers
- **CV review published** — when a BPU recruiter completes a member's expert review
- **Job match alert** — when a new job is posted that matches a Pro member's profile (70%+ match score)
- **Mentor match alert** — when a new mentor is approved who matches a member's profile
- **Booking confirmation** — sent to both the member and the mentor when a session is booked
- **Application confirmation** — sent to the applicant and the employer when a job application is submitted
- **Weekly job digest** — every Monday morning for Pro members who have opted in
